EHRS ABSTRACT

In today’s living migration is one of the Social phenomenon's. People, for various reasons, many a number of times keep moving from place to place. The first requirement and need in a new place is Housing or accommodation. Finding or getting good affordable accommodation in reasonably short time is a challenge. The problem is two ways for both House Owner’s (Searching a Tenant) and house renting community i.e., Tenants community. Though real estate and rental agencies exist, they are expensive or at time biased. It is very difficult for new entrants into a city going door to door in search of rental housing. Searching rental housing involves time, cost and familiarity of the place. Same way for house owners finding tenant involves time, cost and need good social networks. To overcome this complex situation, we have come out with an Automation solution by proposing for development of software system known as “E-HOUSING RENTAL SYSTEM.” EHRS Aims to provide services to “House Owners”, “Migrating population”, “Real Estate Agencies” and other community that directly or indirectly dependent on migrating population. EHRS facilitates various people in the community: 1) Provides house owner’s a platform to inform about their house details to customers (Tenants) that require housing. 2) Facilitates migrants in finding require accommodation in short time and at low cost. 3) Help real Estate agencies in focusing their business areas. We can call this system as House Rental system also.
